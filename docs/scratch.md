# figuring out structure, again (5/26/2025)

## reflecting on previous manual

- liked that the previous manual _had_ structure, though the specific structure felt kinda bleh
  - positives:
    - goal locations were cool
    - structure of needing boss song unlocks up to the victory songs was cool
    - thematic victory songs
  - negatives:
    - boss key requirement was too high
    - too many extras
    - some song locations being outright impossible (in a practical sense) was not a feel-good
    - my choice of boss songs was not the best

## general axioms

- want a more thematic flow that makes use of more SDVX lore and characters
  - specifically by tying character tracks to characters in game
- don't want regular-ass "collect victory resource and beat victory song" structure

## set in stone

- several navigators (SDVX characters featured in cover art)
  - navigators each have their own songs
  - completing navigator song goals give "appeal" for that navigator
  - get enough appeal for a navigator to unlock them
- region-based navigator unlocks
  - navigator region key items shuffled in multiworld
  - each key unlocks _all_ related navigator songs - unlock bigger content chunks vs. dripfeeding progression
- final boss: grace
  - requires a certain number of navigators to unlock
  - inspired by gravity wars mission mode

## open questions

### "explosive" unlocks vs. dripfeed progression

- bigger unlocks:
  - pro: gives more content at once
  - pro: hints matter more
  - con: longer wait times for new content
  - there's the potential of some incredibly important item being locked in someone else's end game (PAPER CURSE)
    - solution: don't have one item be that pivotal to completion
      basically, I should be able to beat the game in logic without any one item
      ⭐ in this manual, I accomplish that by only requiring a certain number of navigators, rather than all of them
- dripfeed:
  - pro: more consistent unlocks (mathematically)
  - con: less content at once
  - con: hints matter less
    - anecdote: time piece and yarn hints NEVER feel good<br>
      this, on top of other things, might just be reason alone that I don't like dripfeed progression
- ⭐ for this, i'll lean towards bigger unlocks
